FModel

FModel is beginner-friendly, open-source software for data-mining games made with Unreal Engine. Its easy-to-understand interface offers complete features, including the ability to create cosmetic icons, which made it popular. FModel supports various loading modes, next-gen data isolation, and can compare two game states to detect new or modified files. It can parse over 30 types of assets, from simple .uasset files to complex .bin files, providing a clear view of all properties.
